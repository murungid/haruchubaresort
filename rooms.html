Your Canopy Sanctuary

Perched 20 feet above the forest floor, our Treehouse Villa offers an unparalleled connection with nature without compromising on luxury.

Built using sustainable materials and designed to blend seamlessly with the surrounding jungle, this 650 sq.ft. retreat features floor-to-ceiling windows, a private balcony with panoramic views, and thoughtful amenities that celebrate the beauty of the natural world.
