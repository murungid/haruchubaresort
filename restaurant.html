The Canopy Restaurant

Elevated jungle cuisine under a canopy of stars

Our award-winning restaurant features seasonal menus crafted by Executive Chef Elena Rodriguez, showcasing the best local ingredients with contemporary techniques. The open-air design allows you to dine surrounded by nature, with the sounds of the jungle providing a unique ambiance.

Our sommelier has carefully curated a wine list featuring the best of Central American vineyards alongside international selections.

Jungle Bar

Where the wild things unwind

Our jungle-inspired bar features signature cocktails made with local spirits, fresh fruits, and herbs from our garden. The perfect spot to unwind after a day of adventure or to enjoy live music on weekends.

Must-Try Cocktails:

  • Howler Monkey Margarita - Tequila, passionfruit, lime, chili salt rim
  • Jungle Bird - Local rum, pineapple, Campari, lime
  • Orchid Mule - Vodka, ginger, hibiscus, lime

Weekly Events:

  • Fridays: Live local music (7-9pm)
  • Saturdays: Mixology classes (5pm)
  • Sundays: Sunset happy hour (4-6pm)
