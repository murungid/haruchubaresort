Culinary Experiences

Taste the

Our restaurant offers truly traditional recipes inherited from our ancestors and also international dishes that can be prepared on order. You can also choose to dine with the founders family to share experiences. Our special beverages include coffee picked from trees aging more than 30 years, handpicked, dried, pounded and ground traditionally for a truly homemade coffee satisfaction. Our herbal teas include wild herbs, lemongrass and other secret grandma's inherited recipes good for your health.

Signature Experience

The Canopy Restaurant

Elevated jungle cuisine under a canopy of stars

Our restaurant features seasonal menus crafted by Executive Chef Elena Rodriguez, showcasing the best local ingredients with contemporary techniques. The open-air design allows you to dine surrounded by nature, with the sounds of the ornamental shade trees providing a unique ambiance.

Our sommelier has carefully curated a wine list featuring the best of Central American vineyards alongside international selections.

Evening Experience

Jungle Bar

Enjoy our evening pleasures at our lively bar with traditional music, reggae, dancehall, country music, and afro beats

Our jungle-inspired bar features signature cocktails made with local spirits, fresh fruits, and herbs from our garden. The perfect spot to unwind after a day of adventure or to enjoy live music on weekends.

Must-Try Cocktails:

  • WARANGI - Matooke homemade gin
  • TONTO - Traditional matooke beer
  • MUKOOBOTI - Made from sorghum and maize flour

Weekly Events:

  • Fridays: Live local music, campfire and traditional dances
  • Saturdays: Movie nights and campfire
  • Sundays: Sunset happy hour (4-6pm)
