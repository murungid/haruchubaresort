Discover orucuba

Orucuba is a rutooro word desribing a well thoughtout meal prepared by many people for very important Guests. In the Batooro culture that guest would be the son in law (omuko).you as our very important guest we provide you with the Batooro cultural program that includes traditional food prepared especially for you

Our eco friendly gardens are live with birds such as sunbirds,tinkabirds,wagtiles,firefinches,turacos,hornbils extrar and others are chameleons,butterflies,bettles,plants such as cycards creating a harmonious balance between indugienious and ornamental shade trees. Whether you seek adventure, relaxation, or simply a connection with nature, orucuba provides the perfect setting.
