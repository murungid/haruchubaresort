Our Story

Where passion for nature meets commitment to sustainable luxury

Haruchuba Resort

A Vision Rooted in Nature

Founded in 2020, Haruchuba Resort was brought to life by Tooro Richard a visionary and the founder of Kabarole Tours Ltd with a mission to create a place where comfort, culture, and nature unite effortlessly.

Driven by his deep love for Uganda and a storyteller’s heart, Richard designed Haruchuba not just as a luxury escape, but as a vibrant base for tourists to explore the heart of the Pearl of Africa. Whether you're relaxing at the resort or venturing out on a guided tour, his desire is simple: to give every guest the best care, the richest experiences, and stories they'll carry for life.

With roots in the tourism world through Kabarole Tours Ltd, Richard brings a rare mix of hospitality and narration where every corner of Haruchuba tells a story, and every stay becomes part of Uganda’s unfolding adventure.

TOORO RICHARD

Founder & CEO
Our Journey

Milestones

Key moments that shaped Haruchuba into what it is today

2010

The Vision Begins

Alejandro purchases 50 acres of secondary jungle with the dream of creating an eco-retreat that would fund conservation efforts in the region.

2020

First Treehouse Built

After three years of careful planning and sustainable sourcing, the first treehouse villa is completed using locally-sourced materials and traditional building techniques.

2022

Eco-Certification Achieved

Haruchuba becomes the first resort in the region to receive Platinum certification from the Sustainable Tourism Institute.

2023

Community Partnership

We establish our "Jungle to Table" program, sourcing 80% of our ingredients from within 50 miles and training local farmers in organic practices.

2024

Conservation Expansion

Haruchuba partners with the National Wildlife Foundation to protect an additional 200 acres of primary rainforest adjacent to our property.

Our Commitment

Core Values

Principles that guide every decision we make

Environmental Stewardship

We go beyond sustainability to actively regenerate the ecosystems we inhabit, using renewable energy, water conservation systems, and habitat restoration projects.

Community Partnership

85% of our staff are from local communities, and we invest 10% of profits into education and infrastructure projects in surrounding villages.

Authentic Experiences

We design encounters that foster genuine connections with nature and culture, from guided wildlife walks to traditional craft workshops with local artisans.

Thoughtful Luxury

Every indulgence is carefully considered - organic linens, artisan-crafted furnishings, and amenities that delight while minimizing environmental impact.

Sustainability

Beyond Greenwashing

Our commitment to the environment is woven into every aspect of Haruchuba, from construction to daily operations.

100% Renewable Energy

Solar panels and micro-hydro systems power our entire resort with clean energy.

Zero Waste

We've achieved 92% waste diversion from landfills through composting and recycling programs.

Reforestation

Over 5,000 native trees planted, restoring 50 acres of degraded land.

Water Conservation

Rainwater harvesting and greywater systems reduce consumption by 65%.
